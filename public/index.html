What is the 20-20-20 Rule?

The 20-20-20 rule says to take a 20-second break every 20 minutes and look at something 20 feet away.
Optometrists recommend this to prevent computer vision syndrome, or digital eye strain, which can cause headaches, dry eyes, and blurred vision.
